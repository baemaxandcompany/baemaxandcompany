What do we wish to achieve at the end of the core (Terry)? Long-term: By the end of the fall semester, we would 1) be setting up agendas to better manage time and be more efficient, 2) be creative with given problems to become innovative intellectuals, and 3) have better knowledge on the general IT industry and information systems to be capable of solving complex problems.
